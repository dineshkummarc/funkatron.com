Nah, I’m not dead — just busy. We finally moved, and we’re still kinda trying to get settled. We also had to buy a new car, which wasn’t bad since the car is BAD-AZZ, but not great since it meant we had a car payment again.

As for development, LameBrain has lately taken a backseat to work on Funkatron Viewer, a simple image viewer intended to sorta emulate the feel of Irfanview, my favorite Win32 image viewer. FV should be ready for release sometime in the next couple weeeks. I do still intend to work on LB, and I have some exciting ideas for it, but I just don’t have the time right now.

This site may be changing in the next few weeks. I want to get more consulting business going, so it’s likely that Funkatron.com will be the address for that. This blog and the apps will still be available, although you might have to click once or twice to find them.
