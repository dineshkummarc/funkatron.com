I haven’t played any videogames in forever. That’s probably what’s been affected the most by having Griffin — I just don’t have much in the way of free time, and lately I’ve been focused on programming. I’d like to game more, but normally I might have at most 10 minutes to play before I’d have to pause to change a diaper/roll him over/clean up vomit. I feel kind of bad about buying the PS2 network adapter, because I only played NFL2k3 online about 4 times. Before that I got maybe 40% into GTA:Vice City, and about 70% through Warcraft III, but I haven’t played any of them in about a month. Hopefully the boy will be able to pick up a controller soon so we can do some bonding by trying to kill each other’s avatar.
