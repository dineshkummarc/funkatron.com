TextMate on OS X has me completely spoiled, and I really really miss a good text editor on Windows.  Much of it has to do with aesthetics, and the fact that the monospaced fonts on Windows suck ass.  Thankfully, I found a cool page that collects links to a bunch of programming-oriented fonts, which will help me escape from the crappiness of Courier New.  The TextMate Wiki also has a good page on alternative fonts for people who are sick of Monaco on OS X (although Monaco is much better than any monospaced font that ships with Windows).

The fonts I’m digging are:

  • DejaVu Sans Mono (has native bold and italic)
  • ProFont
  • ProggyClean
  • MonteCarlo (the bitmap .fon version)
  • Triskweline (the bitmap .fon version)
  • Anonymous
