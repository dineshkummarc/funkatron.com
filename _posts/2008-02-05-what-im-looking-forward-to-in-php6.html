  2. “Safe mode” that disables all output
  3. HDMI outputs
  4. ASCII-art generators added to core (once CLAs are signed)
  5. The quad-colon operator (::::)
  6. All superglobals reduced to single array: $_FUCKO
  7. Improved elegance
