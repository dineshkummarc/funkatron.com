A review was requested.

As I watched this film, I thought of the first couple Batman movies. I never saw the Schumacher-directed ones, because it was utterly clear that they had more to do with Adam West than Frank Miller. So fuck that.

So I thought of the Burton-directed movies, and I came to the conclusion that Tim Burton sucks. Fuck that Penguin shit. Fuck Jack Nicholson. Burton needs to stick to puppet films.

This is the first Batmovie that actually got it. It wasn’t the super best movie I ever saw in my life, but it was pretty fucking good. Nolan and Goyer are gonna get a lot of money to make the next one.
