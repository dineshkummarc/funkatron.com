As you may know, I really really like Pro Football. So today was exciting, because we had the games which determined who would play in next week’s SuperBowl.

I’ve gotten to the point where I can appreciate a good game and a good team without liking or disliking either of the teams involved. Nevertheless, I do tend to favor one team over the other to some extent, especially when the stakes are as high as they were this week. Unfortunately, both my preferred teams (the Tennessee Titans and the Philadelphia Eagles) lost.

Still, I can’t say I’m upset that the teams that won (the Raiders and the Buccaneers) will be in the SuperBowl. Gruden leaving the Raiders and then meeting them the next year in the SuperBowl is just too interesting. This will be a good game, although if I were a betting man, I’d put my money on silver and black — they just seem like a more complete team.
