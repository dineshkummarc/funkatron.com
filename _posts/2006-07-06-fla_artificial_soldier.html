Great album.  hearkens back somewhat to the breakbeat style of Flavour of the Weak.  Jean-Luc De Meyer does guest vox on one song, which is fucking super awesome, and Eskil Simonsson from Covenant does another tune.  It’s certainly not groundbreaking, but it’s great stuff.
