Hootie and the Blowfish Sign Autographs and Perform at Local Wal-Mart:

The band made an appearance to sign autographs at the Wal-Mart Supercenter on Atlantic and Kernan Boulevards around noon today. They gave a preview performance from their new album “Looking for Lucky”.
